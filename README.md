# Lanfriq 🏠

**Fractionalizing African Real Estate with Trust & Transparency.**

Lanfriq is a decentralized platform designed to unlock the potential of African real estate through fractional ownership. By turning physical deeds into digital tokens, we enable anyone to invest in high-value properties for as little as $10.

## 🌟 Vision & Mission

- **Our Vision**: To make real estate ownership in Africa accessible, transparent, and borderless for everyone.
- **Our Mission**: To simplify property investment for millions of Africans by providing a secure platform to verify properties, tokenize assets, and trade real-estate-backed digital tokens effortlessly.

## 🚀 The Problem: The "Locked Wealth" Paradox

- **Accessibility**: 99% of Africans cannot afford to invest in traditional real estate.
- **Liquidity**: Property owners are "land rich but cash poor" – selling can take months or years.
- **Trust**: Fraud and complex paperwork frequently scare away potential digital investors.

## ✅ The Solution: Fractional Ownership & Maximum Liquidity

Lanfriq combines **Legal Compliance (SPVs)** with **DeFi Speed** to provide:

- **Fractional Entry**: Invest with as little as $10.
- **Instant Liquidity**: Trade your tokens on our secondary market.
- **Verified Trust**: Every property undergoes rigorous verification and valuation.

## 🛠 How It Works

1. **Property Verification & Valuation**: Rigorous checks to ensures asset quality.
2. **SPV Generation**: Legal structures are created to hold the property.
3. **Token Configuration**: Defining the fractional units and yield parameters.
4. **Mint + Publish**: Creating digital tokens backed by the physical asset.
5. **Invest & Earn**: Investors buy fractions and start earning rental yields.

## 💰 Business Model

- **Minting Fee**: % charged to property owners to list.
- **Transaction Fee**: % on every trade in the secondary market.
- **Management Fee**: Small % on rental yield distributions.
